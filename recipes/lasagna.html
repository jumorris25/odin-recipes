Lasagna

Lasagna is a pasta-based dish, which usually includes a tomato sauce, ricotta and mozzarella cheeses, and either meat, vegetables, or a combination of both.

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce (we like whole peeled tomatoes)
  • ground beef, or your favorite vegetables
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Mix your tomato sauce with your vegetables and/or ground beef.
  3. Layer the pasta, then tomato sauce mixture, then ricotta. Repeat that layering three or four times, until ingredients are gone.
  4. Generously sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top, and bake for at least 30 minutes.
  5. We recommend two to three minutes under the broiler when baking is done, so the mozzarella gets nice and brown!
